Defence of The Ancients 2

Heroes in Dota 2 consist of a few key components. The assorted stats next to the hero icon, your four spells, health and mana bars, and lastly your six item slots, three backpack slots, and a neutral item slot.

Hero Icon Stats Health and Mana
Going top to bottom the shown stats are:
  • Attack Damage
  • Attack Speed
  • Armor
  • Magic Resist
  • Movespeed
  • Strength Agility Intelligence
    • The top bar shows your current/total health, and your health regeneration.
    Your heroes health is the amount of damage they can take before dying.
    The bottom bar shows your current/total mana, and your mana regeneration.
    Mana is a resource your hero uses to cast their spells.
    Spells Items
    Spells are unique abilities that your hero posesses.
    They can be seperated into active and passive spells.
    Active spells have a key you must press to use them. Passive spells,
    such as the third one shown, are always active and don't need to be pressed.
    The six main item slots are for purchased, usable items.
    If an item is places into one of the three backpack slots, it is unable to be used.
    The two slots on the right are for a neutral item(item dropped from neutral units), and a teleport scroll.
